Good to Know

Before You Reach Out

A few things that help make conversations faster and easier.

Insurance & Eligibility
Many items may be covered under Medicare, Medicaid, or private insurance. Ask us and we can help you figure out what applies to your situation.
Prescriptions & Doctors Orders
Some equipment, like CPAP machines or power wheelchairs, may require a doctor's prescription. Let us know your situation and we'll guide you.
Availability & Lead Time
Stock rotates regularly. Contact us to check on a specific item, and we'll give you an honest answer on availability and any expected wait.
For Caregivers Too
Many of our customers are family members or caregivers buying on behalf of someone else. We're happy to help you find what they need.
Provider & Clinic Supply
We work with clinics and care teams as well as individuals. If you need equipment in quantity, reach out and we'll talk through what makes sense.
No Pressure, No Checkout
We're not a storefront. Ask questions, compare options, take your time. We'd rather help you find the right thing than rush a sale.
